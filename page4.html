About Me

Hello World!
My name is Rasoul Norouzi, an IT master graduated. If you think I am similar to that kind of nerd programmers who only and only concentrate on their works, you are wrong.
I enjoy listening to classical music, playing classical guitar, reading novel books (crazy about Gabriel García Márquez). I consider myself as an outgoing, sociable, dedicated, and a team player person.
Mainly I worked on the text datasets and documents clustering, But inside me, there is a mysterious connection to the math behind the machine learning algorithms. Curiosity pushed me to learner algorithms and AI world, and as I found my passion about it, I have been trying to know how they work and how they will be better.
My focuses in future works and studies will be on below items:

1. Improving Machine Learning algorithms and working with Big Data (mainly tex datasets)
2. Studying about Energy and Stability concepts in Networks.
3. Learning how to develop progressive web applications with embedded AI features in it(future is cloud computing)

I created this web site to teach my learned concepts and share my experience with a wide variety of peoples without regard to boundaries and races. I hope it will be helpful for you.
If you have any question, let me know!
